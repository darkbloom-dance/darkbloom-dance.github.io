## Sparkles Moonbeam

Sparkles Moonbeam is a larger-than-life unicorn with a flair for the fabulous. Born under a double rainbow on a full moon night, Sparkles embodies the spirit of joy, whimsy, and resilience. Known for her gravity-defying moves and infectious positivity, she dances to inspire others to embrace their unique sparkle and to find their joy.
